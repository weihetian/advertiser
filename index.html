CAR ADVERTISING

By using high-mileage car drivers to carry your brand, OOH car advertising allows you to weave your message into the social fabric of the communities that are important to you. With multi-market national reach, carvertise partners with forward thinking brands to engage their target andience in a way thats: Memorable,Targeted, Verificable with Data

Pioneering Brands Marketing With Carvertise

How it works

Each Day hundreds of drivers from around the nation register with our program. They are everyday car owners who want to earn a little extra every month for driving with an ad on their car. In essence, riding with an brand they support and having their commute pay for itself. Each registered drive gives to Carvertise their driving data and car information.

Assembling a Campaign

We start by understanding the specific geographics where our clients need exposure. This can be as large as obtaining a high GRP rating in multiple DMAs, or, as specific as covering a section of a particular city. Using our patent-pending matching algorithm, we then pair our partnering brand with the Carvertisers who drive in the areas of interest

Quality control

Befroe any cars are wrapped, Carvertise runs all matched drivers through the following quality checks.Our strict standards and significant time investment to this process ensure the highest degree of brand image control
